About Me

Hi! My name is João Dias. I'm an aspiring concept and creature designer based in Portugal.
I strive to improve and learn as much as I can. I consider myself a kind of jack-of-all-trades;
if I happen to not know how to do something, it's likely that it's on my to-do list.

In my free time, besides drawing, I dabble in 3D modeling, programming, and writing.

I'm always open to talk so feel free to send me a message to: joao.alexandre.dias1999@gmail.com.
