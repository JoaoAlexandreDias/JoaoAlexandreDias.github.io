Lurer Clam

Dolomamydi klovos


From a distance, this seemingly normal rock is nothing more than that—a rock, yet when inspected in detail, it reveals itself to be a quite efficient ambush hunter.

Using its algae-looking appendages that sprout from its mouth it awaits for prey before snapping itself shut and slowly pushing the prey into its demise.
