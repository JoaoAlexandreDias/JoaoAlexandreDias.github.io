Dungeon Delving


This is the third entry for the second quarter of the contest, Illustrators of the Future.

As for the contest rules I had to create three illustrations that each depicted a diferent subject, as such I decided that for the third one I would focus on the image of a group delving into the depths of a dungeon.


For the creation of this Illustration I used Adobe's Photoshop and Blender.
