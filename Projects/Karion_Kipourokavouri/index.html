Garden Crab

Kipourokavouri aspis

This fascinating creature, no larger than the palm of a human hand, has been observed wandering along the sea floor of Karion.

Its name comes from its apparent habit of "tending" to the surrounding flora—it approaches small algae and slowly "prunes" them with its claws.

Although this behavior may seem strange, it is actually a clever hunting strategy: the creature is searching for its favorite prey, the Protopidacas, which often disguise themselves as these plants.
