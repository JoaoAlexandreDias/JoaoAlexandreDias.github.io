The Blood Moon Massacre


This is the second entry for the second quarter of the contest, Illustrators of the Future.

As for the contest rules I had to create three illustrations that each depicted a diferent subject, as such I decided that for the second one I would focus on the image of a semi-futuristic city and use mostly a red color scheme.


For the creation of this Illustration I used Adobe's Photoshop.
