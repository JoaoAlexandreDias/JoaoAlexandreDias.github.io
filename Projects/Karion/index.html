Karion

Karion is a personal Speculative Evolution Project mostly based on the works of Birrin by Alex Ries and Serina by Dylan Badja.

My objective with this project is to create and evolve the many inhabitants of a planet along the milenia, all this while attempting to keep it in the realm of possibility and plausible scientific acuracy.


The planet that you can see if the planet in question: Karion.
